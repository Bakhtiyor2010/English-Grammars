Past Continuous

Form

Type Structure
Statement (I / He / She / It) + was + verb ing
(You / We / They) were + verb ing
Negative (I / He / She / It) + wasn't (was not) + verb ing
(You / We / They) + weren't (were not) + verb ing
Question Was + (I / he / she / it) + verb ing?
Were + (you / we / they) + verb ing?

Time Expressions

  • at that moment
  • at 7 pm
  • while
  • when
  • all evening

Uses & Examples

Action at a Moment in the Past

  • I was watching TV at 9 pm.
  • She was sleeping.
  • They were studying.
  • We were walking home.
  • He was eating dinner.

Two Actions Happening

  • I was reading while she was cooking.
  • They were talking while we were waiting.
  • He was playing as I was working.
  • We were studying while it was raining.
  • She was drawing while he was listening to music.

Background in a Story

  • The wind was blowing.
  • People were talking loudly.
  • Birds were singing.
  • The sun was shining.
  • Cars were moving slowly.

Watch Out!

We use Past Simple + Past Continuous together when one action interrupts another:

  • The phone rang while I was watching TV.
  • She fell while she was running in the park.
  • We heard a loud noise while we were studying.
  • He spilled coffee while he was reading the newspaper.
  • I lost my keys while I was walking to the store.
