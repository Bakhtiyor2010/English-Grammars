Past Simple

Form

Type Structure
Statement (I / You / We / They / He / She / It) + verb 2
Negative (I / You / We / They / He / She / It) + didn't (did not) + verb
Question Did + (I / you / we / they / he / she / it) + verb?

Expressions

  • yesterday — I watched a movie yesterday.
  • last night — We had dinner at a restaurant last night.
  • last week / month / year — She visited her grandparents last week.
  • in 2020 — I started learning French in 2020.
  • an hour ago — He called me an hour ago.
  • then — We went to the park, and then we had ice cream.

Uses & Examples

Completed Actions

  • I finished my homework.
  • She called me yesterday.
  • We visited Samarkand last year.
  • He broke his phone.
  • They watched a movie.

Repeated Past Actions

  • I walked to school every day.
  • She visited her grandma often.
  • We played football after school.
  • They studied together.
  • He helped his father.

Main Events in a Story

  • The door opened slowly.
  • He looked inside the room.
  • She screamed loudly.
  • They ran outside.
  • The lights went off.
